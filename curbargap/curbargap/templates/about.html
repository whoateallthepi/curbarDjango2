About

curbargap.info was developed to show the data from our 'homebrew' farm weather station. In this intial version you can search weather records for Curbar, as well as some data from previous sitings just down the valley in Grindleford, and Blackheath in London.

I use this data every day on the farm. So I hope it is of some interest elsewhere

You can also view summary and full weather forecasts for the area. These come from the Met Office's 'Datapoint' API. These are something else I use daily.

Coming soon we should have some weather satellite pictures from another DIY project – our NOAA satellite receiver.
